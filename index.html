Visa Information


To explore new worlds has always led the human race to reach new shores.
With the advent of industrialization, each region started having its own passport and visa regulations. A passport is the official document held by an individual that is issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship and entitling them to travel under its protection to and from foreign countries. A visa whereas is a permission document that is issued by a country to a passport holder as an endorsement on a passport indicating that the holder is allowed to enter, leave, or stay for a specified period of time in a country.

More than 16.1 million visas were issued by the Schengen states in 2017.
